Delivery Policy

At Tempo, we aim to deliver your download by email. Please review our delivery terms below.

Order Processing

After payment confirms, a download link is sent to the email used at checkout. Email delivery times depend on the email provider involved and are not guaranteed.

Delivery Timeframes

All products are digital and delivered by email. There is no physical shipping. Customers in any country can access their files after the link is received.

Delivery method: email to the address provided at checkout. No physical shipping.

Email delivery times vary by provider and are not guaranteed. If your link has not arrived within a reasonable window, please contact support.

Delivery Fees

There are no delivery or shipping fees on digital products. The price shown at checkout is the only charge.

Failed or Delayed Deliveries

If your download link has not arrived, please contact help@temporeads.com and we will resend the link or issue a full refund in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.

Incorrect Email Address

Customers are responsible for providing an accurate email address at checkout. If a typo prevents delivery, contact help@temporeads.com with proof of purchase and we will resend to the correct address.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding shipping or delivery, please contact our customer support team at help@temporeads.com.
